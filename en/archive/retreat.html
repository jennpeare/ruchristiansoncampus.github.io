Mid-Atlantic College Retreat

Every year around this time, our club comes together with other Christian sister-clubs from different universities across the New Jersey/New York/Pennsylvania region to spend a weekend fellowshipping and enjoying the Lord together!

Friday, February 19 - Sunday, February 21

REGISTER

Why Attend

Become grounded in your faith

Having been rooted and being built up in Him, and being established in the faith even as you were taught, abounding in thanksgiving. — Colossians 2:7

During your college years, it is important to continue in your walk with the Lord and to become further grounded in your faith. The College Conference provides an atmosphere that will assist you in starting your Christian walk with the Lord at college in a strong and healthy way.

Pursue the Lord with other Christians

But flee youthful lusts, and pursue righteousness, faith, love peace with those who call on the Lord out of a pure heart. — 2 Timothy 2:22

The Bible reveals that the Christian race is not meant to be run alone. At Harvey Cedars, you can meet other students from various schools with whom you will be able to flee and pursue like it says in 2 Tim. In addition to students your age, you can meet and get to know older students and Christian mentors who are all eager to run the race with you and help you along the way.

Discover your destiny in God’s purpose

God placed eternity in man’s heart... — Ecclesiastes 3:11

What is the meaning of life? God has a very definite, clear plan, and you are at the very center of it. Hear what the Bible has to say about what God has been doing throughout history, as never seen before in the Word.

What to Bring

Your Bible, towel and toiletries, pajamas, warm clothes for the weekend and recreational time, tennis shoes

It is not necessary to bring pillows or sleeping bags, as bedding will be provided by the camp.
